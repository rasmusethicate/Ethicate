Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who we are

Ethicate is an independent, citizen-powered platform that allows the public to rate and discuss the ethical conduct of Danish and international companies operating in Denmark. Ethicate is not affiliated with any of the companies listed on the platform.

For questions about this policy or your data, contact us at: [email protected]

2. What data we collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you submit a rating, we store the company, category, star score, and optional source URL you provide. When you submit evidence flags, we store the flag type, source URL, and explanation you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what is necessary to operate the platform.

3. Why we collect it

Your email and name are used solely to create and manage your account, and to allow you to sign in. Your ratings and submissions are used to calculate and display company scores on the platform. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Legal basis

We process your data on the basis of contract (to provide the service you signed up for) and legitimate interest (to maintain the integrity of the platform). We comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Danish data protection law.

5. Where your data is stored

All data is stored on servers located in Frankfurt, Germany (EU), operated by Supabase. Data does not leave the European Economic Area. Supabase complies with GDPR and maintains appropriate technical and organisational security measures.

6. How long we keep your data

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data — including your profile, ratings, and submitted evidence — is permanently deleted within 24 hours. Aggregated, anonymised rating statistics may be retained to preserve platform continuity.

7. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access the data we hold about you, the right to correct inaccurate data, the right to delete your data (right to erasure), the right to restrict processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with Datatilsynet (the Danish Data Protection Authority) at datatilsynet.dk.

You can exercise your right to deletion directly from your account settings, or by contacting us at [email protected].

8. Cookies

Ethicate uses a single session cookie to keep you signed in. This cookie is strictly necessary for the platform to function and does not track you across other websites. We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party tracking.

9. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Ethicate after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Legal notices & takedown requests

All ratings and submissions on Ethicate reflect the opinions and evidence provided by individual citizens. Ethicate does not verify or endorse individual submissions, and ratings are not statements of verified fact. Ethicate benefits from the hosting exemption under the Danish E-Commerce Act (e-handelsloven) and is not liable for user-generated content it did not create, provided it acts promptly upon notification of unlawful content.

If you represent a company listed on Ethicate and believe a specific submission is unlawful, inaccurate, or defamatory, you may request its review by contacting us. We do not yet have a dedicated legal contact but will respond to all requests sent to [email protected] as promptly as possible. Please include the company name, the specific submission in question, and the grounds for your request.

The Problem

In today's world, it is becoming increasingly difficult to act and consume in alignment with your own values. Corporate conduct is complex, often opaque, and spread across hundreds of sources — most people simply don't have the time to investigate.

◈ Evidence-based ratings — every score is traceable to a source
Our Mission

Ethicate exists to change that. We bring together citizen knowledge, public records, and verified evidence to create the most transparent picture of corporate conduct in Denmark — freely available to everyone.

◈ Independent and non-commercial — no company can buy a better score
How It Works

Anyone can rate a company and submit evidence. Ratings are evidence-based, not opinion. Every source is public. Proven violations lock the rating. The result is a living record of corporate accountability — built by citizens, for citizens.
